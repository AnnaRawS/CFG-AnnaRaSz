Box 'n Mind

Welcome to Box ‘n Mind!

Based at Miguel’s Boxing Studio in Loughborough Junction [LINK], our classes take a dynamic approach specifically targeted at building your fitness, technique and mental wellbeing all in one.

The classes combine boxing exercises for 45 minutes with a 15-minute wind-down consisting of relaxation and mindfulness based techniques.

Our classes can be particularly useful for those individuals struggling with their mental health, including depression, anxiety, social anxiety and PTSD. This is a safe space for people to release aggression in a controlled manner and aims to focus the mind.

Our classes are run by Jacqui, a therapist in training with a strong background in clinical psychology. She delivers psychoeducation during the course of the class, instilling tools and techniques to help manage psychological distress and symptoms.
