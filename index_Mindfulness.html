Mindfulness

Mindfulness means knowing directly what is going on inside and outside of ourselves.

Paying more attention to the present moment – to your own thoughts and feelings, and to the world around you – is a proven tactic for improving your mental wellbeing. It can help us enjoy life more and understand ourselves better.

Mindfulness is a core pillar of our Box ‘n Mind classes, but it’s also something you can practice in your own time and on the go.

To learn more about mindfulness you can visit the NHS Moodzone [LINK] and to help incorporate it into your day-to-day try Headspace [LINK], an app that helps you incorporate meditation and mindfulness into your routine.
